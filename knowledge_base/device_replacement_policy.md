# Device Replacement Policy

Battery swelling, smoke, or repeated thermal shutdown requires the user to power off the device immediately and stop charging it.

The service desk must create a safety incident and arrange a replacement shipment within one business day.

If inventory is available, a loaner laptop should be assigned while the damaged device is returned for secure disposal.

Lost or stolen laptops must be reported within 30 minutes so the endpoint team can attempt a remote wipe.
